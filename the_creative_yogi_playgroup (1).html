A Beautifully Structured Journey for Moms & Children

Every 90-minute session balances high-energy play with quiet, restorative focus. To keep our young yogis fully engaged and excited, no two sessions are ever the same. Each class introduces fresh themes, imaginative adventures, and cooperative games that make self-regulation and physical fitness feel like pure play.

Yoga • Art • Mindfulness • Bonding
Our Foundations

The Core Pillars of Our Playgroup

The unique blend of four sensory channels crafted to help kids flourish.

1. Playful Kids Yoga & Storytelling

We translate traditional yoga postures into interactive stories. Your child will stretch, build balance, and improve coordination while pretending to navigate magic islands, climb mountains, or emulate forest animals.

Coordination Storytelling

2. Cooperative Mind Games

Through playful focus exercises like "The Statue of Mindfulness" and tactile guessing games, children develop impulse control, sensory awareness, and deep listening skills in a supportive group environment.

Impulse Control Listening Skills

3. Artistic Self-Expression

Our dedicated drawing segments (such as creating "My Secret Island" or painting collaborative hand-print mandalas) allow children to process thoughts visually, giving mothers a beautiful window into their child's inner imagination.

Creative Art Bonding Window

4. Playful Breathwork & Relaxation

We teach children how to self-soothe using engaging breathing techniques like the Bumblebee Breath and Belly Balloons, concluding each session with a cozy, guided relaxation segment.

Stress Release Guided Recharging

Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 Do I need prior yoga experience?

No, absolutely not! Our poses are translated into fun stories and are extremely gentle, friendly, and adapted perfectly for moms of all health levels and young kids.

🎨 What kind of art materials are used?

We utilize water-soluble, non-toxic, child-safe paints, and markers. Our artistic activities prioritize bonding, sensory exploration, and imaginative play rather than mess.

🚗 Is there parking at Paragon Bay Mall?

Yes! The mall features ample, free secure underground garage parking directly beneath the playgroup venue for up to 2 hours.
